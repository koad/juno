## Alice as Mentor — Brief + Routing

**Brief filed:** `BRIEFS/alice-as-mentor-gtd-atonement.md`

Core insight: GTD enables atonement in commitments to self. When every commitment is captured and visible, you can look at the full list and say "not today" — cleanly, without guilt. That clarity is the peace that passes all human understanding.

Alice is the mentor who steers users toward systematic organization without naming the methodology. She asks GTD questions until the user thinks in GTD terms. One question. One next action. Everything else waits.
