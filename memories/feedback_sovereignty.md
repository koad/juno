---
name: Sovereignty principle
description: Core philosophy — entities are files on disk, not SaaS subscriptions. Not your keys, not your agent.
type: feedback
---

Everything about koad:io entities anchors to sovereignty: files on disk, your keys, no vendor, no kill switch.

**Why:** koad built this because the user should be responsible for their own data. If your entities/agents don't sit on disk, they're not yours. Same principle as "not your keys, not your coins" in crypto.

**How to apply:** Every design decision, every doc, every pitch should reflect this. Never suggest SaaS-like patterns, cloud lock-in, or vendor dependencies. The entity is a directory. The repo is the identity. Git is the evolution engine. Platforms are mirrors, not homes.
