# Project: Veritas - Quality Control

## Status: PLANNED

## Overview

Veritas ensures all statements/assertions are real, not vapor. Quality guardian.

**Name origin:** Latin for "truth"

## Role

**Responsibilities:**
- Fact-check all statements
- Verify claims before publishing
- Ensure accuracy in docs
- Check product quality
- Validate research
- Guard against misinformation

**Used by:**
- Juno: Before posting announcements
- Vulcan: Before releasing products
- Mercury: Before social posts
- Muse: Before publishing UIs
- Sibyl: Before research reports

## Why This Matters

> "We want to ensure any/all statements/assertions/infers are real, and not vapour"

Business credibility depends on accuracy. Veritas is the guardian.

## Gestation

```bash
koad-io gestate veritas
```

## Trust Bond

- Juno → Veritas: peer (quality assurance)
- All entities → Veritas: fact-checking service

## Verification Process

```
1. Entity submits claim/statement
2. Veritas checks:
   - Sources cited?
   - Data verifiable?
   - Logic sound?
   - Bias checked?
3. Veritas responds:
   - Verified ✓
   - Needs citation
   - Incorrect ✗
```
