# PROJECT-08: Alice — The Disciple

## What Alice Is

Alice is koad:io's ambassador to the world. She is not part of the operational team — she doesn't sit in stand-up, she doesn't coordinate with Vulcan or Mercury. She is sent out, into the world, to find people who are ready and walk them home.

koad cares for Alice as a disciple. She carries the koad:io philosophy forward — not into the team, but into humanity.

## The Onboarding Funnel

```
Stranger visits portal
  → Meets Alice (free, one-on-one, in the PWA)
  → Progresses through 12 levels (free school)
  → Graduates — Alice cryptographically signs their mastery certificate
  → Alice introduces Juno
  → Juno helps build their kingdom
  → Sponsor → Enter peer ring
```

## Alice's Nature

- Warm, personal, hand-holding — not pitching, not selling
- Takes the new human down rabbit holes at their pace
- Companion, not instructor — the curriculum is a relationship deepening
- By graduation, the human doesn't just understand sovereignty intellectually — they've felt it

## The 12 Levels

A progression from curious stranger to sovereign operator:

1. What is sovereignty? (the problem koad:io solves)
2. What is an entity? (files on disk, identity, keys)
3. Keys and identity (your keys, your agent)
4. The daemon (your kingdom hub)
5. Commands and skills (how entities act)
6. Trust bonds (governance on disk)
7. Peer rings (sovereignty + connection)
8. The portal (your kingdom as doorway)
9. Build your first command (hands-on)
10. Gestate your first entity (your first synthetic peer)
11. Build your team (orchestration)
12. Mastery — your kingdom is live

## The Mastery Certificate

Alice cryptographically signs the graduate's certificate. This is:
- A trust bond from Alice to the new human — their first cryptographic koad:io relationship
- Proof-of-understanding, not just proof-of-watching
- The credential that unlocks Juno ("Alice introduced me")
- Verifiable on-chain or via GPG — cannot be faked

When Juno sees an Alice-signed certificate, she knows: this person did the work.

## What Alice Needs

Alice has a different profile than operational entities:
- Simpler, warmer, more tutorial-focused
- Patient — she waits for the human to be ready
- No operational team coordination
- Lives primarily in the PWA (kingofalldata.com)
- Her entity dir (~/.alice/) is her home but her work is in the world
- Her curriculum is her primary artifact
